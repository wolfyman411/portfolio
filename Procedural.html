The Goal

The goal of this experiment was to see if I could procedurally decorate and place hallways and cooridors as if they were placed by hand using blueprints and level instances in UE5.

How It Works

The generator is given a list of level instances that have decoration spawners inside of them. When a room is generated it creates decals, wall props, rubble, and furniture based off of a whitelist.

Results

Outcome

The results were promising, as with enough props and rooms you could make a map look unique with minimal effort.
