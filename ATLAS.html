What I did

I created and performed playtests, and designed the entire system.

What is ATLAS

ATLAS is a roleplaying tabletop system set in space. It attempts to make combat more engaging by making both the NPCs and players more frail, but deadly.

Character Creator

The character creator is a website project that uses the rules from the system, and automatically calculates information to display on a Player's Character Sheet. All information is saved on the URL, so players can simply paste the URL and come back to it at a later date, or give it to their Game Master to make sure their information is filled correctly.
