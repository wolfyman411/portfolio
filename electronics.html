Electronics Store

I created an online storefront using a variety of tools and software. The project is made up of three core components, the webpage, the microservice, and the MySQL database. The webpage was made using Javascript and ReactJS, the microservice was built using Java, Spring Framework, and JDBC. I used Postman to make API calls and to test that the webpage was correctly interacting with both the database and the microservice.


Pages

  • Users can log into their personal storefront and add items to their individual cart.
  • If a user wants to wishlist an item they can press the heart button, and it will be displayed on their homepage.
  • Once a user adds an item to their cart, they can checkout. When a purchase is successful, purchased item stock will be reduced from the storefront for all users.

Search

  • Users can search for items in individual categories.
  • The searchbar uses a fuzzysearch algorithm. Rather than forcing the user to type the item correctly, they just need to get most of the letters of product name correctly.

Admin

  • Admins gain access to their own panel not visible to regular users. Here they can adjust or delete users and products.
  • Admins may also upload a CSV to add items in bulk.
  • Order history may also be viewed, allowing admins to filter orders to a specific range and display what items were ordered.
  • Coupons may also be created and given to specific users.

Coupons

  • Before checkout, a user is able to input a coupon they can see on their homepage and discount. The discount is also reflected in order history for admins as well.
