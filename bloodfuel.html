PALADIN

Buffs nearby allies giving them increased health and speed.

ARCHER

Stalks the player and tries to keep a distance from the player. When within range, will fire an arrow at the player.

ORION

Spawns as the boss on wave 10, summons enemies, and fires columns and rows of projectiles.

Phases

Phases control the type of enemies that spawn, the amount of enemies, and how many elites can spawn. These numbers become more aggressive the higher wave the player gets to.

Cards

Cards are awarded to the player after defeating a boss, they can give abilities, attacks, or upgrade the player in some way.

Shop

The player is awarded when they die an amount of death tokens relative to their score. The player can then spend these tokens on lasting upgrades and boons to make the game easier.

Unique Runs

Since cards are randomly chosen from a pool, and players can only pick 2 attacks and 3 abilities, no two runs will ever feel the same.

Complex Spawning

Enemies always try to stick close to the player by spawning and teleporting to the edges of the screen. The amount of elites and number of enemies is determined by a phase that is constantly changing.

Difficult Bosses

At the end of a wave, a difficult boss will spawn, testing the abilities and loadout of the player.
