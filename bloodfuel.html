Bloodfuel

Bloodfuel is an Unity game developed entirely by me. It is a roguelite bullet-hell game with the central mechanic of tying your health with your abilities.


Card System

  • Cards are shuffled before a game starts and dealed out to the player.
  • Cards give the player different abilities and upgrades they can use throughout the run. When a player has 5 abilities and/or attacks, the game will only give upgrade cards.

Wave System

  • Enemies will spawn in defined positions around the player just out of view. These positions follow the player, and ensure that enemies don't spawn in objects or outside the map.
  • Waves are broken into 4 periods that determine how many enemies can spawn, how many elites spawn, and how fast enemies spawn.
  • If an enemy gets too far from the player they will be teleported to a valid spawn position.
  • As the wave number gets higher enemies increase in damage, number, and overall health.

Bosses

  • At the end of a wave, a boss will spawn. These bosses have more complicated attack patterns.
  • Before a boss is visible, specific music tied to the boss allowing the player to better prepare for that specific boss.

Meta Progression

  • During a game, players get points for each enemy killed.
  • On death or game end, the points are converted into tokens that can be used in the shop.
  • Items purchased can be used every run, and give the player additional upgrades to make the game easier.
