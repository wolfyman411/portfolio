Infinite Finality

Infinite Finality is a modification for the game Half-Life 2, featuring unique mechanics, locations, weapons, and enemies.


What I Did

    Ice Axe

    Created a custom melee weapon with prebuilt classes, that features a unique melee charging mechanic.

HoundEye

Custom enemy NPC that fights in groups. I used prebuilt squad systems to increase enemy capabilities based on how many active members were in the current squad.

Bullsquid

Custom enemy NPC that can charge at the player, and fires projectiles in an arc.

Leadership

Along with programming, I was in charge of leading the team. My responsibilities had me creating and delegating task, managing public relations, creating videos for team and public use, keeping asset and programming repositories up to date, onboarding, and handling internal disputes.

Accomplishments

    Community Vote

    Our modification reached 4th place in 2023 for best upcoming mods. In 2024 we were 5th place for "Player's Choice Mod of the Year". Both of these were in competition with thousands of other modifications released during those years.
