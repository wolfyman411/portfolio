Infinite Finality

Infinite Finality is a modification for the game Half-Life 2, featuring unique mechanics, locations, weapons, and enemies. I serve as a programmer and overall leader of the project.


Programming

Bullsquid

  • Bullsquids fire a projectiles in an arc if they are a certain distance away from the player.
  • If the player is too high up, the enemy will fire the projectile in an upwards arc to avoid hitting the ridge.
  • Implemented ramming mechanic that causes the bullsquid to run straight at the player in an attempt to slam into them.

Houndeye

  • Used prebuilt squad systems to interact with how many houndeyes can attack a target at a time.
  • Implemented a feature to make houndeye attacks stronger the more active squadmates there are.

Iceaxe

  • Custom player melee weapon programmed with prebuilt classes.
  • Unique charging mechanic that increases the damage the weapon deals the longer the player holds the attack button.
