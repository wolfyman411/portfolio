The Goal

The goal of the project was to create the cancelled Episode 3 from the critically acclaimed Half-Life 2, and its' subsequent Episode expansions based off of leaks and information gathered from previous Valve developers. While most mods of a similar type attempt to recreate something as if it was in modern day, the goal for this mod was to create something as if it was released on time.

The Team

The team is filled with both experienced and inexperienced developers, coming from a wide range of abilities and time zones. This is a passion project for all of us, meaning nobody was paid to do anything. The team stands at a strong 22 members, and we are still growing even today.

My Job

I am the sole programmer of the mod, as well as the director. I facilitate tasks, ensuring tasks are completed and keeping up to date with volunteers. I am also responsible for arranging meetings, organizing documents, keeping members on track, keeping our scope low, onboarding new members, and prepping for community announcements.

Accomplishments

We came in 4th place on "Best Upcoming Mods" on Mod:DB. This was voted on by the community.

Our channel has reached over 2.5k subscribers, with one of our videos reaching over 85k views.

Our mod has been in development since April 2023, and we are on track to release a fully playable demo by Q3 of this year. This means this will be the one of the first mods in a decade that has attempted this, and actually release something playable to the public.
