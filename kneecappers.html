Character Selection

Players can select characters, each with their own set of abilities and color schemes.

Peer to Peer

All actions are replicated on the host and the client, allowing people to play together without the need of servers.

Engaging Enemies

Enemies wander patrol paths, and scout out for players. Each enemy type reacts differently to spotting a player.

Scoring System

At the end of a game, players are scored based off the objectives they've completed.

Traversal Options

Levels are filled with unique different ways to go throughout the map.
