What I did

I programmed everything in UE5, created all the art assets and integrated them, made music and sound effects, implemented UI, and designed the game.

What is KneeCappers

KneeCappers is a couch co-op heist game, where players take the roles of Mafia members to commit organized crime around a city.

Character Selection

Players can select characters, each with their own set of abilities and color schemes.

Peer to Peer

All actions are replicated on the host and the client, allowing people to play together without the need of servers.

Engaging Enemies

Enemies patrol, and scout out for players, each enemy type reacts differently to spotting a player. Some enemies can also call the aid of nearby allies in a small radius.

Scoring System

At the end of a game, players are scored based off the objectives they've completed.

Traversal Options

Levels are filled with unique ways to traverse throughout the map.
