KneeCappers

KneeCappers is an Unreal Engine 5 game developed entirely by me. It is a sidescrolller co-op heist game where players take on the role as mafia members to commit organized crime around a city.

Peer to Peer

  • Actions are replicated for both players to allow players to play remotely on their own devices.
  • Players can indepently perform actions allowing each member to complete the main objective.

Character Selection

  • Before each level, players can select a character and a skin for that character. This is replicated between players as well.
  • After a time, all players will be sent to a level with their selected characters and skins.

Engaging Enemies

  • Enemies patrol and navigate around the map in an attempt to spot the player.
  • Enemies are broken into classes that use Unreal Engine behavior trees. These classes determine if they'll go into cover, chase the player, or flee.
  • When an enemy is alerted they'll make a call to all nearby enemies in a radius and put them in an alert state to find and attack the player.
