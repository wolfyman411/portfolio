Inventory System

Players have a saving inventory system that using a slot system. Items can be moved around and selected in the inventory. Players can equip an armor, a weapon, and an ability to help them throughout their dungeon diving.

Dungeon Generator

Dungeons are generated uses a specific set of variables based on the mission the player chooses. This can control what types of rooms spawn, the decoration, the enemies, the amount of rooms, what the rooms looks like, and more.

Bounties

Bounties are selected in the main menu, and they control the attributes of the mission, making no 2 missions identical.

Loadout System

Players can pick armor, weapon, and abilities that suit their playstyle.

Enemies

Each enemy is easily distinguishable from the other, and functions differently. Enemy outfits can be controlled by the bounty, allowing them to wear only red armor if needed.

Crafting and Leveling

The difficulty level of the bounty determines entirely on the player's equipment level. The higher the level the more unique bounties the player can take on. Players can upgrade their weapons by using materials they get from scrapping other weapons, or by getting them from chests and enemies.
