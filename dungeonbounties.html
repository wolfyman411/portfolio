What I did

I programmed everything in UE5, created all the art assets and integrated them, made music and sound effects, implemented UI, and designed the game.

What is Dungeon Bounties

Dungeon Bounties is an isometric procedural dungeon crawler, where the focus is about dodging instead of attacking.

Dungeon Generator

Dungeons are generated using attributes based on the bounty the player chooses. This can control what types of rooms spawn, the decoration, the enemies, the amount of rooms, what the rooms look like, and more.

Enemies

Each enemy is easily distinguishable from the other, and functions differently. Enemy outfits can be controlled by the bounty, allowing them to wear only red armor if the developer chooses.

Inventory System

Players have an inventory system that uses slots. Items can be easily moved around and selected in the inventory. Players can also equip an armor, a weapon, and an ability to help them throughout their dungeon diving.

Loadout System

Players can pick armors, weapons, and abilities that suit their playstyle. Each weapon is unique in attributes, and some items may even change how the player interacts with the game.

Upgrading Items

The difficulty level of the bounty determines entirely on the player's equipment level. The higher the level the more unique bounties the player can take on. Players can upgrade their weapons by using materials they get from scrapping other weapons, or by getting them from chests and enemies.

Bounty Selector

Bounties are selected in the main menu, and they control the attributes of the mission, making no 2 missions identical. These can be setup easily using a DataTable and changing the attributes of the bounty.
